The application may allow authentication using your Google Account. When using this feature, some basic account information may be accessed, such as your name, email address, and account identifier, as authorized by the user.

The application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

6. Children's Privacy

The application is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
